    Taša Križnar

    I'm a passionate and creative student with a deep love for art, design, and nature. Currently studying New Media Design at Jönköping University, I’m blending my skills in graphic design, web design, and programming to create innovative digital solutions. I have a strong foundation in tools like Illustrator, Photoshop, Figma, and InDesign, and a keen eye for detail, allowing me to bring ideas to life visually. With over 14 years of volleyball experience, I understand the value of teamwork, dedication, and discipline—qualities I apply in every project I take on. In addition to my design work, I’m committed to animal welfare and have hands-on experience volunteering at animal shelters. In my previous roles, I’ve managed social media for Aleksandra Levov’s Yanika line of pet care products, and I’ve created logos, posters, and ads for various independent clients. I thrive on challenges that allow me to explore creativity, especially when they merge my interests in sports, animals, and the environment. Let’s connect and explore how we can collaborate on creative projects!
